Frequently ask Questions

  • What exactly is Ordermania App?

    • >With this App the restaurant owners can market their menu online and also manage everything from buying process to tracking payments on their smart phone or devices..

  • Are there any security measures taken for this App?

    • Yes.We have reliable security measures taken for this App. We provide security at database level which is very important..

  • Do I need internet connection to use the app?

    • To access exclusive deals and use Mobile Order & Pay, internet connection via WiFi or cellular data is required.

  • Where can I find information about product nutrition and ingredients?

    • To quickly access nutrition & ingredients information, select "More" on the bottom navigation bar, then select "Nutrition," and search for the desired food item within the categories provided. Once you locate the food item, select the product tile. You will find a short description of the product as well as a "Nutrition" tab and "Ingredient" tab. If you have already started your Mobile Order, select the product title of the food item and select the "Nutrition & Ingredients" section, if available.

  • How do I know if Ordermania is available to my address?

    • Menu items will vary by location. For food item availability please select Ordermania App and view items by selecting ‘Order’ on the bottom menu bar.

  • How do I report a problem with my Ordermania order?

    • In order to investigate any issue with an order (wrong delivery address, incorrect items, etc.) and take appropriate steps, we suggest that you contact customer service directly through the Web App.

  • What payment methods you offer in Ordermania App?

    • There are payment options available for easy check out of customers. The other payment method can also be added as per region and client request.
